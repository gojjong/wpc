Jonga's Favorite TV Series Top 5

1/Elite

One of the things I like most about the show is that it's a double narrative story, which mixes adolescent campus love stories with crime and murder stories that are interwoven without being intrusive. The school life in the show is something I have been longing for since childhood, which makes me want to go back to high school.

2/American horror story

I've always liked the framing and narrative pace of horror movies, probably because I liked the grisly atmosphere. Another interesting thing about the show is that every season its has the same, or at least similar, casts. Yet it was telling different stories, and I enjoy seeing the diversity of the actors.

3/Euphoria

The discussion of adolescence, addiction, and indulgence was a good medicine for me at a time when I was confused about life and searching for the meaning of life. I have learned so much from it, and those thoughts always provide me with ideas in my meditations.

4/Black mirror


I like the overall high-tech, near-future atmosphere it creates, and I like the imagination it develops. The fact that these unbridled technologies may indeed appear in our lives offers a new way of thinking about our relationship with technology.
